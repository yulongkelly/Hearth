AI reads and summarizes your inbox. Flags urgent emails, drafts replies — all processed locally, never uploaded.

Calendar awareness

"What's important today?" — Hearth reads your Google Calendar and gives you a smart daily briefing every morning.

Any model, your choice

Zero telemetry

No analytics. No crash reports sent anywhere. No account required. We literally cannot see your data — we have no server.

Mobile access via PWA

Open Hearth on your phone through your home Wi-Fi. Add to home screen — works like a native app, no App Store needed.
